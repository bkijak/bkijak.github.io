INTRODUCTION TO FITNOTFAT

The FitNotFat gym is located in the heart of Norwich also meaning that it is in the heart of Norfolk too. With easily accessible public transport you are able to travel to this location with no difficulties. We offer a wide range of facilities including, but not limited to; sports equipment, changing rooms, restrooms, a cafe if you would simply like to hang out with your friends and vending machines in case you feel thirsty after a hard workout. We offer a range of services including all sort of sports, assistance from our trained staff and space for you to carry out your gym activities in. We have a little bit of everything for everyone, encouraged and motivated by us and the gym you'll surely become "Fit not Fat" in no time.
