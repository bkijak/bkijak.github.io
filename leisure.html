RESTROOMS AND CHANGING ROOMS

In the Fit not Fat gym we have two sets of restrooms for both genders, and another suited for those with disabilities. The restrooms are fully equipped with all the facilities you need; including toilets, sinks, hygienic products as well as towels and hand dryers. We also have two changing rooms (one for each gender) for those people using the gym facilities and separate changing rooms for the people using the swimming pool facilities. All of the changing rooms have many facilities within including lockers, changing stalls, showers, family changing stalls and hair dryers.

CAFE AND RESTAURANT

The café/restaurant are located at the entrance to the Fit not Fat gym. There you can buy snacks and refreshments as well as hot and cold food. Near the café is the bench and table area where you can eat the food that you have bought at the café and socialise with your friends and family before or after workout. The welcoming employees will give you all the information that you need about the range of services they provide and all the products they sell to you. You can also ask special requests for foods that cannot be found on the menu. The restaurant also offers services such as hosting parties and birthday parties for kids; a great combination with swimming!

MASSAGE FACILITIES

Feeling tired or just having a back ache after a long day of working out? Make sure to visit one of our qualified and professional masseuses or masseurs. The peaceful environment of the massage rooms will calm and soothe you. There are many different massages to choose from so make sure to check this facility out! The massage rooms are located near the back entrance of the gym and have a beautiful view on the park which is just right behind the Fit not Fat gym. Depending on the membership type you choose you might be eligible for free massage sessions, for more information on memberships click here.

SAUNA FACILITIES

We also have a sauna available for those who are members in our gym. This new addition is located next to the massage rooms. The sauna area has lots of space for a total amount of 10 people that could can fit in so it’s a perfect place to hang out with your friends and also benefit from the sauna itself. Our employees are going to be there for you whether you need your questions about the sauna answered. Like with the massage facilities, the benefit of being a member of our gym is that you might be eligible for time in the sauna with no extra cost. For more information about memberships click here.
