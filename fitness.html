ZUMBA CLASSES

Zumba is a class tought by a professional Zumba instructor. The Zumba fitness program has music which is mostly made up of Latin music as well as some international music. Zumba class students will have to alternate their workout between fast and slow rhythm music. You will definitely enjoy the Zumba classes if you like to dance to trendy music as well at get fit at the same time!

YOGA CLASSES

Find peace within during our yoga classes which will bring the state of your mind to harmony. These classes are aimed at both beginners as well as experienced yoga students. You should join our yoga classes if would like to gain strength, flexibility, relaxation and balance.

BOXERCISE CLASSES

Boxercise classes include exercise where boxing as well as motivating music comes into play. This is a class for people with all levels of ability. By coming to one of our boxercise classes you will improve your strength, reaction times, flexibility and overall fitness of your body; so if you like boxing make sure to visit one of the available boxercise classes!

INSANITY CLASSES

Insanity classes feature mostly cardio workouts, attending these classes will get you the perfectly toned body you have always dreamed of. These classes will require you to perform your workouts with maximum intensity. Out professional instructor will provide you with assistance and information you need in order to carry out these high intensity workouts correctly and therefore safely.

BOOT CAMP CLASSES

Boot camp classes consist of resistant and cardiovascular training. This class will improve your strength, overall fitness and your reflexes and are also perfect in combination with other sports such as ball sports and racket sports. The instructors for this class are motivating and will provide you with information that you desire.
